Q: What services does Spaeth Tech offer?
A: Spaeth Tech offers three main service areas:
   1. Network Setup & Maintenance – designing, installing, and maintaining reliable, secure networks for homes and offices.
   2. Remote Worker Tech Support – fast, friendly IT help for people working from home, available remotely or on-site.
   3. IT Consulting & Strategy – helping small businesses make smart technology decisions, plan IT roadmaps, and improve security.

Q: Do you help with home networks?
A: Yes! Spaeth Tech sets up and optimizes home networks, including router configuration, WiFi coverage, firewall/security hardening, and troubleshooting. A home network setup project starts at $299.

Q: Do you support small businesses?
A: Absolutely. Spaeth Tech specializes in helping small businesses (1–25 employees) with network setup, workstation configuration, cloud migration, security assessments, and ongoing IT support through monthly retainer plans.

Q: Can you help me set up Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace?
A: Yes. Spaeth Tech handles cloud migrations including Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace setup, email migration, and data migration. Cloud migration projects start at $499.

Q: Do you offer cybersecurity services?
A: Yes. Spaeth Tech provides security assessments covering network and endpoint vulnerability reviews, with a written report and recommendations. Security assessments start at $399. Security hardening is also included in network setup services.

Q: Can you help with device/workstation setup?
A: Yes. Workstation setup includes new device configuration, software installation, and account migration, priced at $149 per device.

Q: Do you do IT audits?
A: Yes. Spaeth Tech offers a full IT Audit & Roadmap service — a complete technology assessment with a written IT roadmap and budget recommendations — starting at $599.

Q: Can you handle custom or unusual IT projects?
A: Yes. Custom projects are scoped and quoted individually at no charge. Contact tyler@spaeth.tech for a free estimate.

Q: Do you offer remote support or only on-site?
A: Both. Remote support is available for all clients. On-site visits are included in higher retainer tiers (Professional and Business plans) and available for project work within 30 miles of Kansas City.

Q: What does "network maintenance" include?
A: Network maintenance includes ongoing monitoring, troubleshooting, performance fixes, router and access point management, WiFi optimization, and firewall upkeep.
